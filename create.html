Questions? We have answers

How are you different from a freelancer or an agency?

We're the best of both worlds. You get the dedicated, personal service and speed of a top-tier freelancer, combined with the structured process, reliability, and polish of a creative agency. We've eliminated the unreliability of the former and the expensive overhead of the latter to create a service that's perfect for decisive business owners who value quality and time.

What do you mean by "single-page" website?

A single-page website contains all its main sections on one long, scrolling page. It's a modern, focused, and effective way to present your business without overwhelming visitors. Think of it as the digital version of a perfect business card.

What if I need more than one page?

Our service is specifically tailored for creating high-impact single-page websites. If your project requires multiple pages (like a large e-commerce store or a complex blog), we might not be the best fit right now. We believe in doing one thing, and doing it exceptionally well.

What about domain and hosting costs after the first year?

The first year is on us. After that, you'll be responsible for the renewal fees. We use reliable, affordable providers, and the cost is typically around S$25/year for the domain and S$120/year for hosting. We'll provide clear instructions on how to renew when the time comes.

Can I update the website myself later?

Yes, you can. We build our sites with clean, high-performance code instead of a slow, complex CMS like WordPress. This is our secret to achieving top-tier speed and security. We provide a few options for updates, depending on your comfort level:

1. For the Non-Technical: Most of our clients prefer to have us handle updates. We offer affordable, pay-as-you-go support for small changes to text, images, or pricing. Just send us an email, and we'll take care of it quickly.

2. For the Tech-Savvy: If you're comfortable with basic HTML, we can give you access to the site's code repository. You can make edits yourself and we'll provide instructions on how to deploy the changes.
