Tuesday, September 2, 2025

INDONESIA

Protests erupt across Indonesia over elite rule

Nationwide demonstrations, initially sparked by anger over lawmakers' housing allowances, have escalated into a violent backlash against what protesters call 'corrupt elites' and policies benefiting conglomerates. The unrest intensified following the death of a delivery driver in Jakarta, leading to widespread riots and the burning of public buildings, posing the most significant challenge yet to President Prabowo's new administration.

Source: The Guardian, South China Morning Post

PM's dismissal plunges Thailand into crisis

Thailand's Constitutional Court has sacked the prime minister, plunging the fragile ruling coalition into chaos and creating a significant power vacuum. This move pushes the nation into a state of political paralysis with no clear successor readily available, raising fears of prolonged instability.
